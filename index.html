"ANUBHUTI" is not just the annual cultural fest of RUIA NSS but a kaleidoscope of emotions, memories, and boundless creativity. This 'Teen Din ka Sapna' is the place where people experience four power packed days of passionate competitions, fun games and activities, events of renowned artists, electrifying concerts and soothing night life. Over the many years that Anubhuti has established its legacy in.
